About Us

Dusk was started in early 2020 as pandemic had just hit. Before got to learn about our application we were ust sharing and posting about the daily news concerning the pandemic. The lack of public attention encouraged us to diversify and be as versatile as possible.

Our breaking point concerned the police brutality that spread around the world concerning an officer beating a kenyan citizen senseless for being a few minutes late past curfew. It was after this that people reached out and wanted to share their stories through continuos blogging.

The rest is what you see here on the page we allow our users to freely express themselves and share that with the world.
